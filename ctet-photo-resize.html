CTET Photo Requirements 2025

CTET (Central Teacher Eligibility Test) CBSE dwara conduct hota hai. Online form me photo aur signature upload karna zaroori hai. Galat size ya format se form reject ho sakta hai.

CTET Photo Specifications:

  • Size: 3.5cm × 4.5cm (413×531 pixels)
  • File Size: 10KB se 100KB
  • Format: JPG / JPEG only
  • Background: White, plain
  • Type: Recent passport style, front facing

CTET Signature Specifications:

  • Size: 3.5cm × 1.5cm (413×177 pixels)
  • File Size: 4KB se 30KB
  • Format: JPG / JPEG
  • Ink: Black ya dark blue
  • Paper: White plain paper

CTET, REET, UPTET, BTET, MPTET – sabhi teaching exams me photo aur signature requirements lagbhag same hain. Ek baar resize karke sab me use kar sakte hain.

CTET Photo Tips

📸 Photo Tips:

  • White background – safed deewar ke saamne khade hokar photo le.
  • Natural light me photo le – flash avoid karein.
  • Face clear aur center me ho – blur nahi hona chahiye.
  • Formal dress – casual kapde avoid karein.

✍️ Signature Tips:

  • Black pen se safed paper par sign karein.
  • Sign ke saath kuch na likhein.
  • ALL CAPS avoid karein – running style me sign karein.
  • Wahi signature karein jo exam hall me bhi karenge.

Common Mistake: Photo me naam/date likhna, signature me CAPS use karna, dark background – ye sab CTET form reject kara dete hain.

CTET Photo Resize – FAQ

CTET photo size kya honi chahiye?

CTET photo 3.5cm × 4.5cm (413×531px), JPG format, 10KB se 100KB honi chahiye. White background, recent passport style.

CTET signature size kya hai?

CTET signature 3.5cm × 1.5cm (413×177px), JPG, 4KB se 30KB. Black ink, safed paper par sign karein.

Kya CTET aur REET me photo size same hai?

Haan. CTET, REET, UPTET, BTET, MPTET, HTET – sabhi me photo 3.5×4.5cm (10-100KB) aur signature 3.5×1.5cm (4-30KB) hai. Ek baar resize karke sab me use kar sakte hain.

CTET photo reject kyun hoti hai?

Wrong file size (10-100KB ke bahar), wrong format (PNG), dark background, blurry photo, face clearly na dikhna – ye common reasons hain.

Kya mobile se li photo chalegi?

Haan. White background me clear photo mobile se le karein aur hamare tool se resize kar lein. Portrait mode use karein.

CTET photo me background kaisa chahiye?

White ya light colored plain background. Dark, colorful ya patterned background allowed nahi. Safed deewar ke saamne photo lein.
