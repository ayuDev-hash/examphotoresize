Railway RRB Photo Requirements – Complete Guide 2025

Indian Railways har saal lakho vacancies ke liye recruitment karti hai. RRB NTPC, Group D, ALP (Assistant Loco Pilot), JE (Junior Engineer), RPF jaise posts ke liye online application form bharna hota hai jisme photograph aur signature upload karna mandatory hai.

Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) ki photo specifications SSC ke similar hain. Photo 3.5cm × 4.5cm dimensions me, JPG format me, aur 20KB se 100KB ke beech honi chahiye. Signature bhi 3.5cm × 1.5cm, JPG me, 4KB se 30KB ke beech honi chahiye.

Bahut se candidates ki application photo upload error ki wajah se process nahi ho paati. Iska sabse common reason hai ki photo ki file size limit se zyada hoti hai ya dimensions galat hote hain. Hamara tool is problem ko instantly solve karta hai.

Good News: Railway ke sabhi exams (NTPC, Group D, ALP, JE, RPF) me photo aur signature requirements same hain. Ek baar sahi photo bana lein toh sabhi Railway forms me use kar sakte hain!

Railway Photo Requirements:

  • Dimensions: 3.5 cm × 4.5 cm (approximately 413 × 531 pixels at 300 DPI)
  • Format: JPG / JPEG only
  • File Size: 20KB se 100KB ke beech
  • Background: White ya light colored plain background
  • Photo Type: Recent color passport photo, front facing
  • Face visible: 60-70% face coverage, both ears visible if possible
  • No cap/hat: Religious headgear exception ke saath
  • No dark glasses: Transparent prescription glasses ok

Railway Signature Requirements:

  • Dimensions: 3.5 cm × 1.5 cm (approximately 413 × 177 pixels)
  • Format: JPG / JPEG only
  • File Size: 4KB se 30KB ke beech
  • Ink: Black ya dark blue ink, white paper
  • Style: Running/cursive – capital letters me nahi

Railway Photo Tips

📸 Railway Form ke liye Perfect Photo:

  1. Studio quality nahi chahiye – mobile se bhi achi photo le sakte hain, bas white background aur achi light ho.
  2. Passport style photo lein – shoulders se upar, seedha dekhte hue.
  3. White deewar ke saamne khade hokar photo lein – sabse easy clean background.
  4. Morning ya afternoon me window ke paas photo lein – natural light best hai.
  5. Camera eye level par rakhein – upar ya niche se photo mat lein.
  6. Plain formal kapde pehne – check ya bright prints avoid karein.

✍️ Signature ke liye:

  1. White A4 paper par sign karein, phir phone se photo le lein.
  2. Dark pen use karein – light ya pencil nahi.
  3. Sign ke around extra white space rakhein – tool baad me crop kar lega.
  4. Running handwriting me sign karein – BLOCK LETTERS nahi.

Dhyan Rakhein: Railway form me photo aur signature dono upload karne hote hain. Agar koi ek bhi galat size me hai toh application submit nahi hogi. Hamare tool se dono ko sahi size me resize kar lein.

Railway Photo – Sawal Jawab

Railway NTPC photo ki exact size kya hai?

Railway NTPC photo 3.5cm × 4.5cm (413×531 pixels), JPG format, 20-100KB honi chahiye. White background zaroori hai.

RRB Group D me signature ki size?

Signature 3.5cm × 1.5cm (413×177 pixels), JPG, 4-30KB. Black/blue ink se white paper par sign karein.

Railway photo me cap pehan sakte hain?

Nahi, kisi bhi tarah ki cap ya hat allowed nahi hai. Sirf religious headgear (turban/hijab) exception hai.

Kya Railway aur SSC me photo requirements same hain?

Haan, dono me lagbhag same requirements hain – 3.5×4.5cm photo, JPG, 20-100KB. Ek baar resize karke dono me use kar sakte hain.

Railway form me photo upload error aata hai, kya karein?

Mostly file size ya dimensions galat hone ki wajah se error aata hai. Hamare tool se resize karein – exact required size me convert ho jaayegi.

Railway ALP aur JE me photo requirements alag hain?

Nahi, Railway ke sabhi exams (NTPC, Group D, ALP, JE, RPF) me photo aur signature requirements same hain.

Kya purani photo use kar sakte hain?

Photo recent honi chahiye – ideally 6 mahine se purani nahi. Railway verification ke time photo match karni chahiye.

Mobile se photo lekar Railway form me upload kar sakte hain?

Haan bilkul! Mobile se white background me photo lein aur hamare tool se resize kar lein. Studio jaane ki zaroorat nahi.
