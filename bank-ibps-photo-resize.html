Bank IBPS Photo Requirements – Complete Guide 2025

Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) aur State Bank of India (SBI) har saal PO, Clerk, SO, Specialist Officer jaise kayi banking exams conduct karte hain. In sabhi exams ke online application form me photo, signature aur left thumb impression upload karna mandatory hai.

Bank exam forms ki photo requirements sabse strict hoti hain kyunki yahan exact pixel dimensions specified hote hain (200×230px for photo, 140×60px for signature). Agar dimensions galat hue toh form accept hi nahi hoga.

Kya Aap Jaante Hain?

Bank exams me photo dimensions pixels me specified hoti hain (200×230px), jabki SSC ya Railway me centimeters me (3.5×4.5cm). Isliye bank form ke liye exact pixels match karna zaroori hai.

Bank Photo ke liye zaroori specifications:

  • Dimensions: 200 pixels (width) × 230 pixels (height) – exact match zaroori
  • File Size: Minimum 20KB, Maximum 50KB
  • Format: JPG / JPEG only (PNG accepted nahi hota)
  • Background: White ya light colored plain background mandatory
  • Photo Type: Recent passport style color photograph
  • Face Coverage: Face photo ka 50-75% area cover karna chahiye
  • Date on Photo: Photo par recent date printed honi chahiye (studio se le)
  • Glasses: Normal glasses ok, dark/tinted glasses NOT allowed
  • Cap/Hat: Sirf religious headgear allowed, koi aur cap nahi
  • Photo Recency: Photo recent honi chahiye – 6 months se purani nahi

Bank Signature ke liye zaroori specifications:

  • Dimensions: 140 pixels (width) × 60 pixels (height) – exact match zaroori
  • File Size: Minimum 10KB, Maximum 20KB
  • Format: JPG / JPEG only
  • Ink Color: Black ink only (blue ink bhi accept hota hai par black recommended)
  • Paper: Safed (white) plain paper par sign karein
  • Style: Running/cursive hand – ALL CAPS me sign na karein
  • Consistency: Wahi signature use karein jo exam hall me, admit card par aur bank documents me karenge

Important Warning:

Bank form me photo par naam aur date VISIBLE hona chahiye (studio wali photo me niche likha hota hai). Lekin signature ke saath kuch bhi nahi likhna chahiye – sirf sign hona chahiye. ALL CAPS me sign karne par form reject ho sakta hai.

Bank Photo & Signature Tips – Professional Quality

📸 Perfect Bank Photo Kaise Le?

  1. Studio photo recommended: Bank form ke liye studio jaake passport size photo le. Studio wale date aur naam bhi print karte hain jo zaroori hai.
  2. Mobile se le rahe ho toh: Safed deewar ke saamne, natural light me, portrait mode me photo le. Face center me rakhein.
  3. Dress code: Formal dress pehnein. T-shirt ya casual kapde avoid karein. Tie zaroori nahi par formal collar shirt best hai.
  4. Expression: Natural expression rakhein. Muskurana ok hai par daant dikhna nahi chahiye.
  5. Ears visible: Dono kaan visible hone chahiye – baal se cover na karein.
  6. Background check: Photo ke peeche koi object ya shadow nahi hona chahiye – clean white background.

✍️ Perfect Bank Signature Tips:

  1. Black gel pen use karein – ball pen se sign patla dikhta hai jo scan me clear nahi aata.
  2. A4 safed paper par sign karein – ruled paper ya colored paper mat use karein.
  3. Sign ke upar-niche-aas paas kuch bhi na likhein – sirf signature hona chahiye.
  4. Mobile se photo le – achhi light me, close-up, crop karke sirf sign rakhein.
  5. Practice karein: Same signature exam hall me, admit card par aur joining time bhi use karni hogi. Ek style fix kar lein.
  6. Capital letters AVOID karein: "RAHUL KUMAR" jaisa sign reject ho sakta hai. Running style me sign karein.

Common Mistakes Jo Bank Form Reject Kara Dete Hain

Ye galtiyan bilkul na karein:

  1. Wrong pixel dimensions: Photo 200×230px ke bajaye kisi aur size me upload karna – form reject hota hai.
  2. File size limit se bahar: Photo 20KB se kam ya 50KB se zyada hona – upload error aata hai.
  3. PNG format upload karna: Sirf JPG/JPEG accepted hai. PNG dene par error milega.
  4. Dark ya colorful background: Red, blue ya dark background wali photo accept nahi hogi.
  5. Blurry/unclear photo: Low resolution ya out-of-focus photo reject hoti hai.
  6. Face bohut chhota ya bada: Face photo ka 50-75% hona chahiye – na bahut door se, na bahut paas se.
  7. Signature me CAPS use karna: "RAHUL KUMAR" jaisa sign invalid hai. Cursive/running style me karein.
  8. Signature ke saath naam likhna: Sirf sign chahiye – niche kuch na likhein.
  9. Purani photo use karna: 6 months se purani photo mat use karein. Recent photo lein.
  10. Sunglasses ya cap: Dark glasses aur cap (religious ke alawa) allowed nahi hain.

Left Thumb Impression Guide

IBPS aur SBI ke zyada tar exams me photo aur signature ke saath left thumb impression bhi upload karni hoti hai. Ye ek additional requirement hai jo bank exams ko unique banati hai.

Left Thumb Impression Specifications:

  • Dimensions: 240×240 pixels (square)
  • File Size: 20KB se 50KB
  • Format: JPG / JPEG
  • Ink: Blue ya black stamp pad ink

Thumb Impression Kaise Le?

  1. Blue stamp pad khareedein (stationery shop se).
  2. Safed plain paper par left thumb ka impression le.
  3. Clearly pressed karein – halka mat rakhein, na bahut zyada dabayein.
  4. Fingerprint ki lines clearly visible honi chahiye.
  5. Mobile se photo le aur crop karke square shape me rakhein.
  6. Hamare tool me "Custom Size" select karke 240×240px, 50KB max set karein.

Left Hand ka Thumb: Baayein haath (left hand) ka angootha (thumb) use karein. Right hand ka nahi. Agar left thumb me koi problem hai toh right thumb use karein aur form me mention karein.

Banking exam photo se related sabhi common questions ke detailed answers

IBPS PO me photo ki exact size kya honi chahiye?

IBPS PO me photo 200 pixels (width) × 230 pixels (height) honi chahiye. File size 20KB se 50KB ke beech honi chahiye, sirf JPG/JPEG format me. Background white ya light color hona chahiye.

Ye same specifications IBPS Clerk, IBPS SO, IBPS RRB PO/Clerk me bhi apply hoti hain.

IBPS/Bank signature ki exact dimensions kya hain?

Bank exam me signature 140 pixels (width) × 60 pixels (height) honi chahiye. File size 10KB se 20KB ke beech, JPG format me. Black ink se safed paper par sign karein.

SBI PO aur IBPS PO me photo size same hai kya?

Haan! SBI PO, SBI Clerk, IBPS PO, IBPS Clerk – lagbhag sabhi bank exams me photo 200×230px (20-50KB) aur signature 140×60px (10-20KB) chahiye. Specifications almost identical hain.

RBI Grade B, RBI Assistant, NABARD, LIC AAO me bhi same specs hain.

200×230 pixels ka matlab kya hai? Ye kitni badi photo hai?

200×230 pixels bahut chhoti photo hai – approximately 2.5cm × 2.9cm screen par. Isliye file size bhi kam hoti hai (20-50KB). Ye passport photo se chhoti hoti hai.

Comparison: SSC photo 413×531px hoti hai jo kaafi badi hai. Bank photo usse almost aadhi hai.

Bank photo me background kaisa hona chahiye?

White ya light colored plain background mandatory hai. Dark colors (red, blue, black), patterns ya outdoor backgrounds accept nahi hote.

Best option: Photo studio me jayein aur white backdrop ke saamne photo khichayein. Ghar par bhi safed deewar ke saamne le sakte hain.

Left thumb impression kaise upload karein?

IBPS/SBI forms me left thumb impression bhi upload karni hoti hai:

  • Blue stamp pad se left hand ke thumb ka impression safed paper par lein
  • Fingerprint lines clearly visible honi chahiye
  • Mobile se photo le aur crop karke square shape me rakhein
  • Dimensions: 240×240 pixels, file size: 20-50KB, format: JPG

  • PNG format upload kiya (sirf JPG chahiye)
  • Dark background
  • Blurry/out-of-focus photo
