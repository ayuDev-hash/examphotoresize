SSC Photo Requirements – Complete Guide 2025

Staff Selection Commission (SSC) har saal CGL, CHSL, MTS, GD Constable, CPO, Stenographer jaise kayi exams conduct karta hai. In sabhi exams ke online form me photo aur signature upload karna mandatory hai.

Agar photo ya signature ki size, dimensions ya format galat ho toh form reject ho sakta hai ya upload error aa sakta hai. Isliye exact specifications follow karna bahut zaroori hai.

Important: SSC ke sabhi exams (CGL, CHSL, MTS, GD, CPO, Steno) me photo aur signature ki requirements almost same hain. Niche table me details diye gaye hain.

SSC Photo ke liye zaroori baatein:

  • Size: 3.5 cm × 4.5 cm (approximately 413 × 531 pixels at 300 DPI)
  • Format: JPG / JPEG only
  • File Size: 20KB se 100KB ke beech (exam notification ke hisaab se)
  • Background: White ya light colored plain background
  • Photo Type: Recent passport style, front facing
  • Face Coverage: Face photo ka 60-70% hona chahiye
  • Glasses: Dark glasses allowed nahi, normal transparent glasses ok
  • Cap/Hat: Religious headgear ke alawa koi cap allowed nahi

SSC Signature ke liye zaroori baatein:

  • Size: 3.5 cm × 1.5 cm (approximately 413 × 177 pixels)
  • Format: JPG / JPEG only
  • File Size: 4KB se 30KB ke beech
  • Ink Color: Black ya dark blue ink
  • Paper: Safed (white) plain paper par sign karein
  • Style: Same signature jo exam me use karenge

Warning: Photo me naam ya date NAHI likhna chahiye. Signature ke niche bhi kuch nahi likhna chahiye. Capital letters me sign mat karein.

SSC Photo & Signature Tips

📸 Photo Achi Kaise Le?

  1. White background ke saamne khade ho jayein – safed deewar best hai.
  2. Natural light me photo le – window ke paas best location hai.
  3. Front facing straight dekhein – sar na jhukayein, na ghumayein.
  4. Mobile ka portrait mode use karein for better quality.
  5. Photo sharp aur clear honi chahiye – blur photo reject ho sakti hai.
  6. Formal dress pehne – casual ya fancy kapde avoid karein.

✍️ Signature Tips:

  1. Safed (white) paper par black ya dark blue pen se sign karein.
  2. Sign ke upar niche kuch nahi likhein – sirf signature hona chahiye.
  3. Capital letters me sign na karein – running/cursive style me karein.
  4. Photo achi light me le aur crop karke sirf signature rakhein.
  5. Wahi signature use karein jo exam hall me bhi karenge.

SSC FAQ

SSC Photo Resize – Aksar Poochhe Jaane Wale Sawal

SSC CGL me photo ki exact size kya honi chahiye?

SSC CGL me photo 3.5cm × 4.5cm (approximately 413×531 pixels) honi chahiye, JPG format me, 20KB se 100KB ke beech.

SSC signature size kitni honi chahiye?

SSC signature 3.5cm × 1.5cm (approximately 413×177 pixels) honi chahiye, JPG format me, 4KB se 30KB ke beech.

Kya SSC CGL aur CHSL me photo requirements same hain?

Haan, SSC ke lagbhag sabhi exams me photo aur signature ki requirements same hain. Upar table me sabki details hain.

SSC photo me background kaisa hona chahiye?

White ya light colored plain background. Koi pattern ya dark background nahi hona chahiye.

SSC form me photo reject kyun hoti hai?

Common reasons: wrong file size, wrong dimensions, dark background, blurry photo, face not visible clearly, wrong format (PNG instead of JPG), photo me naam ya date likha hua.

Kya mobile se li photo SSC form me chalegi?

Haan, mobile se li photo chalegi. Bas ye ensure karein ki photo clear hai, white background hai, aur hamare tool se resize kar lein.

SSC photo me glasses pehan sakte hain?

Transparent/normal glasses ok hain. Dark sunglasses allowed nahi hain. Glare free photo lein.

Ek baar resize ki photo SSC ke sabhi exams me use kar sakte hain?

Haan! SSC CGL, CHSL, MTS, GD, CPO – sabhi me same specifications hain. Ek baar resize karke save kar lein.
