BPSC Photo Requirements 2025

BPSC (Bihar Public Service Commission) Bihar me civil services, teacher (TRE), judicial service aur anya exams conduct karta hai. Online form me photo aur signature upload zaroori hai. Galat size se form reject ho sakta hai.

BPSC Photo Specifications:

  • Dimensions: 3.5cm × 4.5cm (413×531 pixels at 300 DPI)
  • File Size: 20KB se 50KB (exam notification check karein)
  • Format: JPG / JPEG only
  • Background: White ya light color, plain
  • Photo Type: Recent passport style, front facing, ears visible
  • Recency: 3 mahine se purani nahi honi chahiye

BPSC Signature Specifications:

  • Dimensions: 3.5cm × 1.5cm (413×177 pixels)
  • File Size: 10KB se 30KB
  • Format: JPG / JPEG
  • Ink: Black ya dark blue pen
  • Paper: Safed (white) plain paper par sign
  • Style: Running/cursive – ALL CAPS avoid karein

BPSC ke alag-alag exams (PT, Mains, TRE, Judicial) me photo size thodi vary ho sakti hai. Hamesha latest notification check karein. Is tool me common BPSC requirements preset hain.

BPSC Photo & Signature Tips

📸 Photo Tips:

  • White background – safed deewar ke saamne photo le.
  • Natural light use karein – direct flash avoid karein.
  • Ears dikhne chahiye – baal se cover na karein.
  • Formal dress pehnein – casual kapde avoid.
  • 3 month se recent photo use karein.

✍️ Signature Tips:

  • Black gel pen se safed paper par sign karein.
  • Sign ke saath kuch bhi na likhein.
  • CAPITAL LETTERS avoid – running style me karein.
  • Wahi sign karein jo exam hall me bhi use karenge.

Avoid: Dark background, blurry photo, PNG format, 50KB se badi file, signature me naam likhna – ye sab BPSC form reject kara dete hain.

Pro Tip: Ek baar hamara tool use karke photo resize kar lo – BPSC PT, Mains, TRE sabme same photo use kar sakte ho (agar 3 month ke andar ho).

BPSC Photo Resize – FAQ

BPSC photo size kya honi chahiye?

BPSC photo 3.5cm × 4.5cm (413×531px), JPG format, 20KB se 50KB honi chahiye. White background, recent passport style photo.

BPSC signature size kya hai?

BPSC signature 3.5cm × 1.5cm (413×177px), JPG, 10KB se 30KB. Black ink se safed paper par sign karein.

70th BPSC me photo size kya thi?

70th BPSC me photo 3.5×4.5cm, ≤50KB JPG thi. Upcoming BPSC exams me bhi similar ya same requirements expected hain.

BPSC TRE Teacher photo size kya hai?

BPSC TRE (Teacher Recruitment Exam) me photo 3.5×4.5cm, ≤50KB aur signature 3.5×1.5cm, ≤30KB, JPG format me chahiye.

BPSC photo reject kyun hoti hai?

Wrong file size (50KB se zyada), wrong format (PNG), dark background, blurry photo, face clearly na dikhna – ye common reasons hain. Hamara tool ye sab automatically handle karta hai.

Kya mobile se photo BPSC form me chalegi?

Haan. White background me clear photo le, portrait mode use karein, aur hamara tool se exact size me resize kar lein.

BPSC Judicial me photo size alag hai kya?

BPSC Judicial Service me photo size thodi zyada allowed hai – ≤100KB tak. Hamara tool me "BPSC Judicial" option separately diya gaya hai.
